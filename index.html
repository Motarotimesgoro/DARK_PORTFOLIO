Conquering the Mind: Overcoming Impostor Syndrome in Tech

Impostor syndrome is a pervasive issue in the tech industry, where professionals often feel like they don't belong or aren't deserving of their accomplishments. This mental barrier can hinder creativity, reduce productivity, and lead to burnout. Many talented individuals, from seasoned developers to budding designers, experience the anxiety of being "found out" as frauds despite their evident skills and achievements.
